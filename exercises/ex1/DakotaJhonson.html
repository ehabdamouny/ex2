Dakota Johnson

The Daughter

Dakota Mayi Johnson (born October 4, 1989) is an American actress and model. The daughter of two actors Melanie Griffith and Don Johnson.
She made her film debut at age 10 with a minor appearance in Crazy in Alabama (1999), a dark comedy film which starred her mother. Johnson was discouraged from further pursuing acting until she completed high school, after which she began auditioning for roles in Los Angeles.
